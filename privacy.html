Privacy

How Lume treats your data.

This is a human-readable summary you can keep while you work on a full legal policy. Replace it with your official text when you’re ready.

What we collect

Lume may access data you choose to share, such as step counts, activity, and other health metrics from HealthKit, along with basic app usage analytics.

We also store information you provide directly, like challenge names, goals, and friend connections.

How we use it

Your data is used to power core features: showing trends, challenges, reminders, and insights about your movement, sleep, and water intake.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Sharing & friends

When you join challenges or add friends, Lume may share limited activity stats (like steps or challenge progress) with the people you connect with. We never share sensitive details such as exact locations.

Your choices

You can disable permissions (including HealthKit access) at any time in iOS Settings. If you uninstall the app, locally stored data is removed from your device.

For account or data questions, contact your support email and we’ll help.
