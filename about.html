About Lume

Lume was built by Alex Manning, an independent iOS developer passionate about creating tools that help people live healthier, more intentional lives.

Why I Built Lume

I wanted a single, beautiful place to see all my wellness data without the clutter. Most health apps are either too clinical, too complicated, or only track one thing. Lume brings together steps, sleep, water, mindfulness, and habits into one calm, card-based dashboard that actually feels good to use.

What Makes Lume Different

Lume was featured as Apple's App of the Day. It connects with iPhone, Apple Watch, Oura Ring, Garmin, and Whoop so you can use whatever devices you already own. The AI-powered trends and science-backed suggestions help you understand your data without needing a degree in data science.

The social features, including challenges and friend tracking, make wellness something you do with the people you care about, not just by yourself.
